Why Ghana

More Than Just a Beach

Ghana's coast blends natural beauty with rich Akan culture, warm hospitality, and incredible seafood — all without the crowds.

  • 🌊

    Unspoilt Atlantic Shores

    Miles of undeveloped coastline with warm water year-round, perfect for swimming and surfing.

  • 🐢

    Wildlife & Eco Tourism

    Leatherback and olive ridley sea turtles nest along Ghana's western beaches from October to March.

  • 🥁

    Vibrant Culture

    Local drumming, kente cloth markets, and fresh grilled tilapia all come with the coastal experience.

  • ✈️

    Easy to Reach

    Accra's Kotoka International Airport connects to major global hubs, making Ghana an accessible destination.

When to Go

Best Times to Visit

☀️

Dry Season

Nov – Feb
Peak beach weather. Sunny skies, calm seas, and ideal for swimming. Best for first-time visitors.

🌊

Surf Season

Oct – Apr
Consistent Atlantic swells make Busua and Kokrobite ideal for surfers of all levels.

🐢

Turtle Season

Oct – Mar
Sea turtles nest on western beaches. Join guided night watches at Cape Three Points.

🌿

Green Season

May – Sep
Lush landscape and fewer tourists. Some rain, but great value and local festivals.
