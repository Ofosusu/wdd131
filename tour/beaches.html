Practical Info

Before You Go

🚌 Getting Around

Hire a private driver from Accra for day trips, or take shared taxis (tro-tros) to coastal towns. The road to Busua via Takoradi is well maintained.

💵 Costs

Beach entry fees at major beaches like Labadi are typically GHS 30–60. Budget accommodation near Busua starts from GHS 200/night. Fresh seafood meals rarely exceed GHS 100.

🌡️ Weather

Ghana sits 5–10° north of the equator. Sea temperatures stay around 26–28°C year-round. Humidity is higher May–October. Sunscreen and light clothing are essential.

⚠️ Safety

Atlantic currents can be strong, particularly at unsupervised beaches. Always swim where locals swim, and heed any warning flags posted. Travel insurance is strongly recommended.
